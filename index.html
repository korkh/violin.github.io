OUR SCHOOL

“Do you want to learn how to play music but you don’t have the skills or the time? Well.. We are here for you!”

Jessica Antoin Chackyan, VioliN's music school director.

"Violin" is an online music school for all ages. We have every think that you have been looking for. We can teach you how to play piano, guitar and violin following a practical new teaching method. You can learn by reading sheet note and if you find it hard you can choose to learn how to play instrument by number.

All the courses are recorded so you get to follow up what have you missed at your own time. If you had any troubles in the way our dedicated teacher is here to help. Don’t hesitate to reach out. We want music to bring you pleasure first of all! And if your hobby develops into a professional activity, then that's great!
